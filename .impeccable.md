## Design Context

### Users
Photography enthusiasts, potential clients, and tech professionals browsing a personal portfolio. Visitors arrive from social links (Instagram, Unsplash, LinkedIn) or direct traffic. They're casually browsing on any device, often on mobile. The job to be done: appreciate the photography, understand who Wei Khang is, and possibly reach out.

### Brand Personality
**Clean, Calm, Refined** — The portfolio should feel like a quiet gallery, not a loud marketplace. Let the photography be the hero. The UI recedes, the images speak.

**Emotional goal:** Calm appreciation. Visitors should feel unhurried, like wandering through a well-curated exhibition. No urgency, no flashiness.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Minimal, restrained, gallery-like. Inspired by Unsplash's clean grid and Tanco Camera's elegant simplicity (tanco-camera.com) — images are the hero, UI is invisible, generous whitespace.
- **Theme:** Light and dark mode, both fully supported. Neutral palette (no pure black — use neutral-900/950). Blue-to-purple gradient accent for brand identity.
- **Typography:** Signika as the single brand font (weights 400-700). Nothing You Could Do for rare decorative accents only.
- **Anti-references:** Avoid busy layouts, excessive animations, trendy glassmorphism, or anything that competes with the photography. No neon colors, no gradients on everything.

### Design Principles

1. **Photography first** — Every design decision should serve the images. If a UI element draws attention away from the photos, remove or tone it down.
2. **Quiet confidence** — Use restraint. Fewer effects, more whitespace. Subtle hover states over dramatic animations. The site should feel effortless, not try-hard.
3. **Consistent neutrals** — Stick to the neutral palette (neutral-50 through neutral-950). The only color should be the blue-purple brand accent and social brand colors on hover.
4. **Respect the viewer** — Support dark mode, reduced motion, screen readers. Don't autoplay, don't trap scroll, don't block content behind animations.
5. **Performance is a feature** — Cloudinary auto-format, lazy loading, small bundles. Fast load times are part of the calm experience.
